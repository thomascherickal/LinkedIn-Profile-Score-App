How to Export your LinkedIn Profile as a PDF

  1. Open your web browser, go to LinkedIn, and navigate to your Profile page.
  2. Find the header box containing your name and headline. Click the More button (positioned next to "Add profile section").
  3. Select Save to PDF from the dropdown menu.
  4. LinkedIn will instantly compile your structured resume and save it as a PDF. Drop or upload it below!
